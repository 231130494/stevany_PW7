    • Bali Bird Park

    Bali Bird Park (Indonesian: Taman Burung Bali), is a tourist attraction in Bali, Indonesia. It is located at the Gianyar Regency and has an area of 2,000 square metres (0.49 acres).
    The Bird Park houses more than 1,300 birds representing more than 250 species in an enclosed aviary.

    Tiket Bali Bird Park : Rp. 150.000 / orang
